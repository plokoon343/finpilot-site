Good to know

Frequently asked questions

Is Automonie free?

Yes — creating an account and using the core features is free.

Which banks can I import from?

Any Nigerian bank statement exported as CSV, Excel, or a digital (text) PDF. Scanned image PDFs aren't supported.

Is my data secure?

Traffic is encrypted (HTTPS), passwords are hashed, and you can enable email two-step verification. Card details are never stored in full.

Do the web and mobile apps share data?

Yes. One account works across both — your data stays in sync.

How do I install the Android app?

Tap “Download for Android”, open the file, and allow installation. No Play Store needed for the beta.
