Built to be trusted

Your money data, protected

Automonie is designed so that the sensitive parts of your finances stay out of our hands in the first place.

We never see your bank login

Bank connections run through a licensed open-banking provider. You sign in directly with your bank — your credentials never touch our servers.

Read-only access

A linked bank account can only be read, never debited. No one — not us, not anyone with your account — can move money out of your bank through Automonie. Disconnect any time.

Encrypted end to end

Every request is protected with 256-bit TLS encryption. Passwords are stored only as one-way salted hashes — they can't be read or recovered, even by us.

Locked to your device

Add two-step email verification, plus a PIN or fingerprint lock on Android, so your finances stay private even if someone else picks up your phone.

We don't sell your data

No advertisers, no data brokers. Your financial information is used to run the app for you — nothing else. You can export or delete it whenever you want.

Licensed partners

Payments, wallets and bank data are handled by regulated Nigerian providers — so the money side is run by licensed institutions, not by us alone.

Read the detail in our Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.

Good to know

Frequently asked questions

Is Automonie free?

Yes — creating an account and using the core features is free.

Which banks can I import from?

Any Nigerian bank statement exported as CSV, Excel, or a digital (text) PDF. Scanned image PDFs aren't supported.

Is my data secure?

Traffic is encrypted (HTTPS), passwords are hashed, and you can enable email two-step verification. Card details are never stored in full.

Do the web and mobile apps share data?

Yes. One account works across both — your data stays in sync.

How do I install the Android app?

Tap “Download for Android”, open the file, and allow installation. No Play Store needed for the beta.

If it's free, how does Automonie make money?

The core budgeting, importing and goal-tracking features are free. In future we'll offer an optional paid plan with advanced features, and we earn small margins on services you actively choose to use — like paying a bill or buying airtime in the app. We never sell your data.

Can I delete my data?

Yes. You can delete your account and its data at any time from the app, and disconnect a linked bank account instantly. You can also export your transactions. See the Privacy Policy for your full rights under the Nigeria Data Protection Act.

Can Automonie move money out of my bank account?

No. Linked bank accounts are strictly read-only — we can see transactions to keep your records current, and nothing more. Money only moves when you explicitly initiate it from your Automonie wallet.
